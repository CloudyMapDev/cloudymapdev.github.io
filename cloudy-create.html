Cloudy - Creating a World

Tip! If you do not have it, you can use /gui in order to get a main menu tool to manage worlds.
Creating a world is very easy.
First, you open the gui and click on the Worlds button.
Click on the green Create World button.
Click on the green confirm button to confirm you want it.
All good! Enjoy your world.

Tip! The default world names are your username. If you create more, it has a -2, -3, etc after the name!
Don't like the name? Go ahead and rename it!
/world rename [old name] [new name]
You can also use spaces in the new name.
Be very careful. You must put the world in quotes if it has a space and a command asks for it.
